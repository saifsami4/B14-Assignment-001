Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the hackathon? +
Any registered DevConf attendee can join — students, professionals, and first-time hackers are all welcome. No prior hackathon experience required.
Do I need a team, or can I join solo? +
Both are fine. You can register with a pre-formed team or join our team-matching session at kickoff to find teammates on the spot.
What can I build? +
Any project is welcome, but bonus judging points go to projects that use tools or APIs from our sponsors. Full track details will be shared at kickoff.
Is there a cost to participate? +
No — the hackathon is free for all DevConf ticket holders. Meals, snacks, and workstations are provided for the full 48 hours.
How will projects be judged? +
A panel of engineers from our sponsor companies and DevConf speakers will score submissions on creativity, technical execution, and real-world impact.
